Five Scenarios

  1. Where Can I find another business like mine?

    The site provides a directory where you can find a list of business

  2. How can I join this chamber?

    With a little fee, anyone can signup and join

  3. How can I find a supplier?

    The list of businesses is in the directory. However, you can post it on a wall for a specific needs

  4. I need a business partner, is the chamber can help?

    This chamber is your business partner. You can access contacts in different ways, from support, from the company list, from the people list, etc.

  5. Do I need promotion for my products?

    Yes, you can announce it here
