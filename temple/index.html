WELCOME TO THE LORD'S CASTLE

Striking accommodation in the heart of the city

We're a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. We cater to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. Our overall goal is to provide information about the specialized services that this hotel chain offers to meet the needs of temple patrons who come to serve in the temple or who participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips.
